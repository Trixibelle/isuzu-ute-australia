INTRODUCING THE ISUZU MU-X

A true family-sized seven-seat SUV, the Isuzu MU-X redefines the boundaries of adventure. Whether you’re navigating the weekday school run or traversing the wildest weekend, MU-X has you covered for the routine, extreme, and everything in between.

  • Seven-seat comfort:.Whether you're doing a sports run or heading for adventure, the 7-seat Isuzu MU-X has plenty of room and features to keep your family entertained.
  • 3 tonne towing:.MU-X’s powerful Isuzu turbo diesel engine gives you an impressive 3 tonnes+ of towing capacity. You can take your trailer, boat or caravan on any adventure you want.
  • Off-road adventure:.With the Terrain Command 4x4 system and robust yet refined suspension, you can drive MU-X into the toughest terrain with ease. Wherever your spirit takes you, the Isuzu MU-X is along for the ride.
  • Fuel efficient SUV:.MU-X has a highly-efficient Isuzu diesel engine that uses leading technology to provide precise fuel control. So it does more work and burns less fuel — saving you money.
